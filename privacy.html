Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 23, 2026

CoolKat is built with your privacy at the core. We believe your health data belongs to you — and only you. This policy explains what data the app uses, how it's stored, and your rights.

Data We Store

CoolKat stores only the data necessary to provide you with a great weight tracking experience.

Weight Entries
Your daily weight logs, including date and value
Stored on device & iCloud
Goal Data
Your target weight and progress preferences
Stored on device & iCloud

iCloud Sync

CoolKat uses Apple's CloudKit to sync your weight data across your devices. This data is stored in your personal iCloud account and is governed by Apple's privacy policy. We do not have access to your iCloud account or its contents.

Important: Your weight data is stored in your private iCloud database. Only you can access it. CoolKat's developers cannot view, read, or retrieve your personal data.

Data We Do NOT Collect

CoolKat does not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Personal identification (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device analytics or usage tracking
  • Health data from Apple Health
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Cookies or web tracking data

No Account Required

CoolKat is designed to work without any login or account creation. You never need to provide an email address, phone number, or any personal information to use the app.

No Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third parties. Your data stays on your device and in your personal iCloud account. There are no analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or third-party trackers embedded in the app.

No Advertising

CoolKat does not display advertisements of any kind. We do not use advertising identifiers or participate in ad networks.

Data Retention & Deletion

All your data is stored locally on your device and in your personal iCloud account. You can delete your data at any time by:

  • Deleting individual weight entries within the app
  • Deleting the app from your device (removes all local data)
  • Managing your iCloud storage through your device settings (removes synced data)

Children's Privacy

CoolKat is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Since the app requires no account and collects no personal data, no special provisions are necessary.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy as new features are added to CoolKat. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
